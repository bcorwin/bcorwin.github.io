HI, I’M BEN

I’m a software engineer, data scientist, and development technologist based in Vermont who works remotely across the US. I have extensive development expertise across enterprise application products and data pipelines. I take a collaborative approach to utilizing data and statistical tools to give quantitative support to cross-functional stakeholders across diverse industries.

I’ve worked with companies of all sizes from start-ups to massive national retailers, synthesizing data and using statistical tools to help inform business decisions and build applications. To put it simply, I’m experienced, detail-oriented, and can communicate effectively with every member of your team.

In my spare time, I’m a prolific tinkerer and avid learner — I almost always have a side project in progress. From board games to book clubs to hiking as many National Park as I can with my wife, I’m always up to try something new both on and off the job.
